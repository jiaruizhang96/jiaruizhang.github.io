About Me

Thank you for visiting my website! I am a dedicated machine learning engineer with over three years of experience in supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, NLP, and recommender systems. After earning my Bachelor's degree in Engineering Science with a major in Machine Intelligence from the University of Toronto, I am currently pursuing a Master of Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My professional journey includes significant contributions at Huawei Technologies Canada's Human-Machine Interaction Lab, where I worked on several speech recognition projects to enhance user experience. My research interests have led me to work on projects like high-speed video reconstruction using diffusion models and multilabel classification for computational pathology.

Passionate about continuous learning and innovation, I strive to leverage my skills in Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and AWS to tackle complex challenges and create impactful solutions. I am committed to advancing the field of machine learning and look forward to building meaningful technologies that benefit society.
